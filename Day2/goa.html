Welcome to Goa

Goa, the party capital of India, is known for its sandy beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. Explore three of its must-visit destinations below.

Calangute Beach

Known as the "Queen of Beaches," Calangute Beach is perfect for water sports, sunbathing, and vibrant shacks serving local Goan cuisine.

Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this 16th-century church houses the remains of St. Francis Xavier and showcases exquisite Baroque architecture.

Dudhsagar Falls

One of India's tallest waterfalls, Dudhsagar Falls cascades magnificently through lush greenery, offering a breathtaking sight.
