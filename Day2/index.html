Rohtang Pass, Manali

Explore the breathtaking views of the Himalayas at Rohtang Pass, a must-visit destination in Himachal Pradesh.

Sri Harmandir Sahib (Golden Temple)

A sacred place for Sikhs, this architectural marvel in Amritsar is renowned for its serene atmosphere and spiritual significance.

Taj Mahal, Agra

The iconic white marble mausoleum, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, is one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

KERALA

Known for its backwaters, beaches, and hill stations. Famous places include Munnar, Kochi, Alappuzha, and Thekkady.
HIMACHAL PRADESH

Famous for its hill stations, temples, and adventure sports. Popular destinations include Shimla, Manali, Rohtang Pass, and Dharamshala.
UTTAR PRADESH

Known for its historical monuments and religious sites. Famous places include the Taj Mahal (Agra), Varanasi, Mathura, and Lucknow.
PUNJAB

Known for its rich Sikh history, agricultural landscapes, and vibrant culture. Famous places include Golden Temple (Amritsar), Jallianwala Bagh, and Wagah Border.
RAJASTHAN

Known for its deserts, forts, and palaces. Popular places include Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jaisalmer.
MAHARASHTRA

Famous for its heritage, beaches, and hill stations. Popular spots include Mumbai, Pune, and Ajanta Caves.
UTTARAKHAND

Known for its spiritual and natural beauty. Popular destinations include Rishikesh, Mussoorie, and Nainital.
KARNATAKA

Famous for its ancient ruins, beaches, and wildlife. Popular places include Hampi, Mysore, and Coorg.
WEST BENGAL

Known for its cultural heritage and scenic beauty. Famous places include Kolkata, Darjeeling, and Sundarbans.
GOA

Famous for its beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. Popular places include Panaji, Calangute, and Anjuna.
