Welcome to Karnataka

Karnataka, the land of heritage and natural beauty, offers a mix of modern cities, historical ruins, and serene hill stations. Explore three iconic destinations below.

Bengaluru

Known as the "Silicon Valley of India," Bengaluru is famous for its tech hubs, vibrant nightlife, and beautiful gardens.

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Hampi boasts magnificent ruins, ancient temples, and surreal landscapes, making it a must-visit.

Coorg

Known as the "Scotland of India," Coorg offers misty hills, lush coffee plantations, and serene waterfalls.
