Welcome to Kerala

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is a stunning state in southern India renowned for its serene backwaters, lush greenery, and diverse culture. Explore some of the most popular tourist destinations below.

Munnar

Munnar is a beautiful hill station famous for its sprawling tea plantations, misty hills, and pleasant weather. It's an ideal destination for nature lovers and honeymooners. Don't miss the Eravikulam National Park and Anamudi Peak while you're here.

Alappuzha (Alleppey)

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is famous for its enchanting backwaters and houseboat cruises. It offers a unique experience of serene canals, lush paddy fields, and picturesque villages. The annual Nehru Trophy Boat Race is a major attraction.

Kochi

Kochi, also known as Cochin, is a vibrant port city with a rich history and cultural heritage. Explore the iconic Chinese fishing nets, Mattancherry Palace, and Fort Kochi. The city's blend of colonial charm and modernity makes it a must-visit.
