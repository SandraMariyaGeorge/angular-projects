Welcome to Maharashtra

Maharashtra, a vibrant state in western India, is home to bustling cities, ancient caves, and scenic hill stations. Discover its wonders below.

Mumbai

Known as the "City of Dreams," Mumbai is famous for Bollywood, the Gateway of India, and its vibrant culture.

Ajanta and Ellora Caves

These UNESCO World Heritage Sites feature ancient rock-cut caves showcasing exquisite Buddhist, Jain, and Hindu art.

Lonavala

A picturesque hill station, Lonavala is perfect for nature lovers, offering misty hills, waterfalls, and scenic trekking trails.
