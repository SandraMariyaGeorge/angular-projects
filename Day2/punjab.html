Popular Places in Punjab

Golden Temple

Located in Amritsar, the Golden Temple is the holiest Sikh shrine, known for its stunning architecture and spiritual significance.

Jallianwala Bagh

A historical garden in Amritsar commemorating the tragic massacre of 1919, an important site in Indian history.

Wagah Border

The Wagah Border is famous for its daily ceremonial parade conducted by Indian and Pakistani soldiers.
