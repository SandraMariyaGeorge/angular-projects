Welcome to Rajasthan

Rajasthan, the "Land of Kings," is known for its majestic forts, vibrant culture, and expansive deserts. Explore its iconic destinations below.

Jaipur

Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is famous for its stunning architecture, including Hawa Mahal and Amber Fort.

Jodhpur

Known as the "Blue City," Jodhpur is renowned for the magnificent Mehrangarh Fort and its blue-painted houses.

Udaipur

Known as the "City of Lakes," Udaipur offers stunning palaces, tranquil lakes, and romantic sunsets.
