Popular Places in Uttar Pradesh

Taj Mahal

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love located in Agra.

Varanasi Ghats

Varanasi, the spiritual capital of India, is known for its sacred ghats along the River Ganges.

Bara Imambara
