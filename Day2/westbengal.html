Welcome to West Bengal

West Bengal is a blend of culture, nature, and history, offering everything from serene mountains to bustling cities. Discover its attractions below.

Kolkata

Known as the "City of Joy," Kolkata is famous for its colonial architecture, vibrant festivals, and intellectual legacy.

Darjeeling

Known for its tea plantations and the iconic toy train, Darjeeling offers stunning views of the Himalayas.

Sundarbans

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Sundarbans is home to the Royal Bengal Tigers and a unique mangrove ecosystem.
